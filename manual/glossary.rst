.. glossary::

    Vertex
        a point in 3D space
        A point is defined by its 3 axis coordinates x,y,z. 
        In blender the z-axis points *upwards*, 
        hence when you are in top view, 
        you see the x-y plane (x from left to right, y from bottom to top)
